Von Uyvico

I code and make music.


Von Uyvico, known by his stage name Protocleus, mainly produces EDM subgenres, such as Bigroom, Progressive House, Future Bounce, Dubstep, Trap, and Complextro. Apart from EDM, he also puts his hands onto producing classical music such as orchestrated remixes of popular songs.

The stage name Protocleus has been inspired by Von Uyvico's birthplace, Philippines. The name Protocleus started going public since December 31, 2017. His musical journey didn't start there, he started producing music when he was 10 from a simple yet fully-functional application called Caustic 3 to a very popular most-preferred digital audio workstation, "FL Studio."
